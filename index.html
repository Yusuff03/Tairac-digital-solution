What We Do

We specialize in helping individuals and organizations with seamless registration of their business, company, or NGO with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in Nigeria.

Our Packages

  • Business Name Registration: Ideal for small business owners and entrepreneurs.
  • Company Registration: For limited liability companies and incorporated businesses.
  • NGO Registration: For non-profit and charitable organizations.

Each of the above services comes with Silver, Gold, and Diamond package options to suit your needs.

Simple Process

  1. Click "Get Started"
  2. Select your preferred registration type
  3. Choose your package
  4. Click on Send Requirements to fill the registration form and upload your documents manually
  5. Click the link below the registration form on WhatsApp to proceed to payment page
  6. Receive your receipt instantly and confirmation via WhatsApp
